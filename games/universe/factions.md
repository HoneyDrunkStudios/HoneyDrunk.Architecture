# Universe — Factions

**Status:** Seed (placeholder; canon accretes — keep loose)
**Last updated:** 2026-06-20

The "civilized" powers of the world — the human (and non-human) organizations competing in the contest for power. Danger in this world comes not only from monsters and entities but from **factions with real geopolitical, economic, and social differences** (`overview.md`).

## Principles (locked) — detail (TBD)

- **Every faction is self-justified.** Each has a coherent worldview in which it's the reasonable one. No "evil empire" archetypes; grey morality is structural (`overview.md`).
- **Factions compete for essence *and* territory/safety.** Some are essence-climbers; some primarily protect the ordinary majority's stability (the two-tier society). Most real factions are a mix.
- **Factions hoard knowledge.** Secret techniques and the true rules of power are competitive assets (`power-system.md`, "knowledge as power").
- **Factions relate to the pantheon.** Some are effectively houses/cults competing for divine favor (`pantheon.md`).
