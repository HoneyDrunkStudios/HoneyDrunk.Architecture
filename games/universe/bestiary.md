# Universe — Bestiary (Monsters as People)

**Status:** Seed (placeholder; canon accretes — keep loose)
**Last updated:** 2026-06-20

The monsters and creatures of the world. The defining principle here is a worldbuilding rule, not a flavor note.

## Principle (locked): monsters are people too

**Man-eating monsters are not evil — they're a species surviving.** They eat, raise young, hold territory, and want to live the lives they want, same as anyone. To humans they're predators; to themselves, **humans are the invasive apex predator.** This is the clearest expression of the universe's grey, perspective-dependent morality (`overview.md`).

## How monsters fit the cosmology

- They eat people **for essence** — metabolism, not malice (`cosmology.md`).
- They may **embody specific essence types**, making them sources to hunt/acquire from (the "take it" acquisition path).
- They compete for **territory** like the factions do — another front in the contest for power.
