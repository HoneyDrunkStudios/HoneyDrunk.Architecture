# Title — The Dream Game (codename TBD)

**Status:** Vision (not in production; target end of the ladder — PDR-0012)
**In-universe:** Yes
**Last updated:** 2026-06-20

> This is the north-star title. It is deliberately **not** game 1, 2, or 3. It exists here so the ladder has something to build toward; expect it to evolve as the earlier games teach us what's real.

## Elevator pitch

A faction-driven, fully-explorable, choice-reactive **open-world RPG**. A grounded modern city that opens into other realms/dimensions. You are a customizable protagonist who starts **extremely weak** and climbs by *understanding* a rule-based, typed-essence power system — in a world where everyone (factions, monsters, entities, deities) is locked in an endless contest for power.

## Pillars

1. **Urban-fantasy frame** — modern city baseline + passage into other realms (loose "gates" vibe, not a literal mechanic).
2. **Rule-based, typed-essence power system** — legible, costed, exploitable; the soul of the game (`universe/power-system.md`).
3. **Zero-to-hero climb** — start genuinely weak; the fantasy is the climb.
4. **Pacts & borrowed power** — a layer, not the core (player must be able to grow strong without it).
5. **Living world of factions** — geopolitical/economic/social frictions; danger from people, not just monsters.
6. **Vast, fully traversable world** — mountain peak to seafloor, every terrain.
7. **Agency** — choices matter; play how you choose; customizable protagonist.

## Spine (candidate — not locked)

Leading candidate: **the climb — becoming powerful by *understanding* power, starting from nothing.** Alternative under consideration: **agency** (your choices reshape the world; power is one expression). To be locked when this game enters real design — pillars 5–7 are what make it dream-scale.

## What this game needs (and which ladder rung proves it)

| System | Proven by |
|---|---|
| Ability/combat (typed essence) | Game 2 |
| Dialogue, branching choice/consequence, factions, pacts | Game 3 |
| World streaming, traversal, realm transitions | Game 4 |
| Full open world + deep faction simulation (too big to prototype) | Built here, at scale |

## Big open decisions (defer)

- Combat presentation: action vs. turn-based (operator open to either; dislikes hybrid; smaller games may differ — the dream game picks what proved best).
- Scale of open world vs. handcrafted density.
- The spine (above).
- Single-player only (assumed yes unless revisited).
